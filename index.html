Literacy Programs

Our Success Coaches are here to be your advocate and help you manage your college experience. From academics to your job search, we understand the needs of adult learners who need support in their college journey. Success Coaches are a vital source at Shorter College, helping students grow intelligently, creatively and personally

Success Coaches

Shorter College believes in second chances. We are proud to be one of only two institutions of higher learning in the state to offer a Path to Possible to Arkansans who are currently incarcerated. The critical role we play in assisting the state as it works to return men and women back to their communities with education and skills to live as productive citizens is an important one. Through participation in the national Second Chance Pell Grant Program, Shorter offers incarcerated men and women an opportunity to achieve their goal of earning a college degree.

SECOND CHANCE PELL PROGRAM

Offering assistance to students at every phase of the academic journey is important to Shorter College. We understand that support helps you stay on your Path to Possible, and if you need assistance with coursework, help is just a visit away! The Literacy Advocacy Program offers tutoring services to help Shorter students improve their reading, writing, and math skills at all stages of their college experience.
